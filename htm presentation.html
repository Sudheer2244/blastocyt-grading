Embryo Grading System

Deep Learning for Blastocyst Quality Assessment

A Clinical Decision Support Tool

üìã Problem Statement

Due to increasing stress, heavy change and environmental factors, infertility cases are rising globally. 40-45% for women under 35, dropping to 10-20% for women above 40.

Challenge

Manual embryo grading is subjective, time-consuming, and requires extensive expertise from embryologists.

Impact

Inconsistent grading leads to suboptimal embryo selection, affecting IVF success rates and patient outcomes.

Solution

AI-powered automated grading system providing objective, consistent, and rapid embryo quality assessment.

üéØ Project Overview

Quality of cells that form the fetus

TE
Trophectoderm

Quality of cells that form the placenta

EXP
Expansion Grade

Blastocyst expansion level
